1. Download and install Any Video Converter Free.

(avc-free.exe)

2. Open Any Video Converter.

3. Add the video you want to convert.

4. Click the button just beside the Preset Output message.

5. Choose the video output that you want.

6. Click Convert All Now!

7. Repeat for each video format.

-----------------------------
These are the formats to which to convert. 

HTML5 > .ogv
HTML5 > .webm
HTML5 > .mp4
Flash > .swf
Flash > .flv
